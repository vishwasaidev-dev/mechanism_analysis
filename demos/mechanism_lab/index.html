Mechanism Lab

A polished 2D mechanism viewer for exploring classic mechanisms and recombining them into new concepts. First pass is intentionally lightweight: browser-only SVG, no Blender required.

Why this helps

We can prototype mechanism ideas as clean 2D simulations first, then graduate good ones into Blender/CAD later.
